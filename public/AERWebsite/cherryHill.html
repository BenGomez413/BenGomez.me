FAQ

For your convenience, answers to the most common questions we receive:

Q: What is an Escape Room?

A: An Escape Room is a live-action game starring you and your friends, as you solve puzzles, complete challenges, and decipher clues to complete the mission and escape the room!

Q: How old do I have to be to play?

A: Anyone can play, but players under the age of 18 need an adult to sign the waiver on their behalf and players under the age of 10 must be accompanied by an adult throughout the game.

Q: When can I schedule an escape game?

A: Games must be booked at least 30 minutes in advance or rescheduled 24 hours in advance. We suggest that you arrive at least 15 minutes prior to your booking time to fill out our wavier, and ensure you get the full hour in your room.

Q: Where can I find the waiver?

A: Our waiver can only be accessed in person in our lobby.

Q: What happens if we can’t solve any of the puzzles?

A: During your game, you can ask for clues from the Game Master, so don’t panic if you get stuck!

Q: Do you issue refunds?

A: We do not issue refunds for booked games, but you may reschedule by calling in.

Q: What if we don’t fill all the slots for a room?/ Will other people be in the room with us?

A: Our rooms are by default public. Empty spots can be booked by other customers. If you'd like a private experience with a smaller team give us a call and we may be able to accommodate you.

Q: Can I take pictures inside the room?

A: Photographs and video recording are not allowed in the rooms. We put a lot of time and effort into the design and construction of our rooms, so we don’t want to spoil anything for players who haven’t gone yet.

Q: What happens if I am late for my scheduled Escape Room experience?

A: Customers will not be allowed to join a game that is in progress! That means if you’re with other players and they have started you will be unable to join, so make sure to arrive 15 minutes early!

Q: Where are my pictures?

A: We upload your pictures every Monday and Friday to our Facebook!
